Our Philosophy

Less Business, More Family

Here at TAPS, we believe that customers should be more than just customers. We want to create a business with loyal customers rather than more. The quality over the quantity, family over dollar signs. Since we're still a business however, we also value our family's money and time. We do the best we can to make sure our people are satisfied with what they're paying for. Efficency is key to any great business so we do our best to make sure that no one is waiting around in line for too long and that all appointments are done on time.

Meet the Owners

Todd Vo

As an immigrant, hair has beem a way for my father to adjust to life in America. When I think of something that connects people, I think of hair. Everyone gets their hair cut, no matter what background they may come from. I believe that we're running more than just a business at TAPS.

Phong Nguyen

When I was younger, I used to work at my parents' nail salon every summer. I wanted to own a business that could surpass theirs which led to the birth of TAPS. With years of experience under my belt, I decided to open up my own salon to continue down the path that my family has already walked.

Madam Mochi

One of TAPS' core ideologies is to provide our customers with a comfortable atmosphere. As the company mascot, I work hard to maintain that atmosphere while also acting as a source of friendliness and calmness for the customers when business calls for it.
